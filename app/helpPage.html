Tsunami Safe Help Page

Tsunami Safe is a web app that uses your current location to plot the fastest route to a civil defence safe zone. In order for this to work, you must have a gps enabled device, and you must have allowed your web browser to use your gps location. For more information on enabling gps services on your device, please consult your device manual or manufacturer's website.

On the map there are several things one must take note of:

  • The blue dot in the blue circle is your current approximate position
  • The blue line represents the fastest route to the nearest safe zone
  • The red area is the area that should be avoided at all costs in the event of a tsunami
